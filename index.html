School Auditorium

was built in 1982

The school auditorium stands as a versatile hub within the campus, serving as a focal point for various events and activities. With its spacious layout and tiered seating, it accommodates both academic presentations and artistic performances with ease. The stage, equipped with advanced sound and lighting systems, provides a platform for students to showcase their talents and express themselves creatively. This space fosters a sense of community by hosting assemblies, concerts, debates, and theatrical productions, enriching the educational experience beyond the confines of the classroom.
